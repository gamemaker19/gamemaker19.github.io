Agent Stickman

A sinister conspiracy threatens the nation and its stick citizens. Your country needs you, Agent Stickman!

Play as Agent Stickman through 10 unique levels in this action-packed platform shooter.

Brandish over 20 weapons and command 3 different vehicles to investigate the conspiracy.

Immerse in a variety of different playstyles, such as gun-blazing action, spy-agent stealth and ninja-like platforming, depending on the current level and mission objectives.

Team up with a buddy who plays as "Agent Sidestick" in co-op single player missions.

Battle it out with other agents in up to 24-player multiplayer.

Please note: These features are projected for a possible completed version. As of now, only the first level exists as a prototype.
